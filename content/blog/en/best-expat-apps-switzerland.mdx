You need to understand health insurance, local transport, documents, residence registration, banking, and often a new language at the same time. That is why having the right mobile apps makes a real difference.

These are useful, but they usually solve only one small problem at a time. The real challenge is understanding how all the important tasks fit together when you are building a new life in Switzerland.
